About TechnoX

Founded in 2015, TechnoX is a college club focused on fostering technological innovation and entrepreneurship among students. Our mission is to empower students to turn ideas into impactful projects and startups.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We encourage out-of-the-box thinking and foster a culture of creativity and problem-solving.

Collaboration

Teamwork is at the core of everything we do. Together, we build stronger, smarter solutions.

Impact

Our projects aim to make a real-world difference by addressing pressing challenges in society.

Our Alumni

Over the years, TechnoX has produced a number of successful alumni who have gone on to lead tech companies, start their own businesses, or work in influential roles at global organizations.
